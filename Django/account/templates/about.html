About GADDHE

Government App to Detect & Decrease Holes Efficiently

At Gaddhe (Government App to Detect and Decrease Hole Efficiently) a pothole detecting app .We strongly believe to provide better governance and enhance public participation which will facilitate flow of information among the concerned stakeholders. It will help citizens, authorities and elected representatives to interact and help each other to resolve their issues .We provide a comfortable and friendly user experience at an amazing speed.
